Import from BoardGameGeek


This process activates the unique BGG API url for your collection.
If your collection is sufficiently large the built-in timer won't be long enough
for BGG to prepare their response. If that is the case you will be redirected
back to the admin page. Please wait several minutes and try again.

Note: Depending on the size of your collection the import can take several minutes.
